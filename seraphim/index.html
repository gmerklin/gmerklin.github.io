Musicians

The Seraphim Singers welcomes seasoned musicians from a variety of cultural and religious backgrounds. The ensemble consists mostly of organists, composers, and other professional musicians whose knowledge and experience nourishes a passion for arresting and demanding repertoire.

About Us

We present sacred choral music as a living tradition that reaches from the earliest notated works through the present day. Performing new works by living composers is integral to this mission and we have presented many acclaimed premiere performances of works by local composers.

Press + Accolades

Our 2016–2017 season was a success and garnered praise from critics and audience alike. Following our 20th Anniversary concert on April 30, Seraphim friend and concertgoer Richard B. commented: “You paint with such an expressive palette, achieving subtleties and tremendous contrasts of dynamics and color. Every single piece was delectable, a true feast for the heart, mind, and ears.”
