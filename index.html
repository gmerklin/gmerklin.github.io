Welcome

Church Musician Services is a full-service interim and consulting church musician business.

We offer to churches of all sizes interim services as organist, choir director, music director or combination. We also provide consultation on the musician search process, from defining needs and goals to writing job descriptions, advertisements and contracts to assisting with interviewing and auditioning processes.
