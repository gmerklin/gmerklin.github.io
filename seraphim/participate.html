Unpaid Opportunities

Sing with Seraphim: Interested in singing with Seraphim? Come to our open rehearsal and sing through the repertoire for the November concerts, get to know some of the singers, and find out what Seraphim is all about!

We seek singers with previous choral experience, good sight-reading skills, ability to control vibrato and good intonation.There are openings in all voice parts, particularly tenors and basses.

Sign up in advance to attend the open rehearsal on Monday, Sept 9, with auditions to follow, or you may audition by appointment over the summer. Bring a solo of your choice, and expect to do a little sight reading. Rehearsals are Monday evenings, 7:00-9:30, usually at Church of Our Saviour in Brookline. The upcoming season concerts will be Nov 9 and 10; December 21; February 29 and March 1; and May 11.
